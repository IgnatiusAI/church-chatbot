Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry provides opportunities for all middle school and high school students to meet together and study the Scriptures, learn about the history and traditions of Christianity and enjoy time with their peers.

Sunday Youth Formation

Sunday School: Middle school and high school students meet together at 10:00 a.m. each week in Classroom 222 of the Parish House.

EYC Sunday Evenings will meet regularly in the Trinity Center:

  • Middle School EYC meets from 5:00-6:15
  • Dinner for Middle and High School from 6:15-6:45
  • High School EYC meets from 6:50-8:00

Weekday Bible Studies

  • Middle School Girls: Tuesday mornings at 7 a.m. at Bruegger’s Bagels
  • High School Girls: Wednesday mornings at 7 a.m., at Bruegger’s Bagels
  • High School Boys: Wednesday evenings at 7:15 p.m., rotating locations among the boys’ homes
  • Middle School Boys: Thursday mornings at 7:25 a.m., at Citadel Park
