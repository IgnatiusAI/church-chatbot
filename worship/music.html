Music

The choirs of Trinity Cathedral offer praise to God through leadership in music and liturgy. They are also known as a leading choir in the Anglican tradition and have sung services and concerts all over the world.

Hear Sacred Music in Worship

We offer several opportunities to worship each week with our amazing choir. We would love to see you in-person. Choral services are also live-streamed.

Choral Evensong
From September through May, we offer Choral Evensong (a service of readings, prayers, and music) each Sunday at 4:00 p.m. and on select weekdays. These services are free and open to all.

Choral Compline
During Advent and Lent, join us in the Cathedral for a service of Choral Compline at 8:00 p.m. each Thursday. These services are free and open to all.

Bach Vespers
featuring the North Carolina Baroque Orchestra. Bach Vespers are held in the Cathedral at 4:00 p.m. These services are free and open to all.

  • January 21 - Cantata 155 
    Mein Gott, wie lang, ach lange 
  • February 11 - Lobet Den Herrn, BWV 230

Friends of Music at Trinity Cathedral allow us to undertake the special projects that breathe life into our program, making it compelling and vibrant for children and adults who participate.
