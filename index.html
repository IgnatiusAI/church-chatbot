Adult Ministry

Trinity offers groups for men, women, young adults and college students. Whatever stage of life you’re in, our goal is to help you grow in your faith.

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry provides opportunities for all middle school and high school students to meet together and study the Scriptures, learn about the history and traditions of Christianity and enjoy time with their peers.

Children's Ministry

Children’s Ministry at Trinity Cathedral provides programs and opportunities for all children to study the Scriptures, history and traditions of Christianity and the Episcopal Church.

Music Ministry

The choirs of Trinity Cathedral offer praise to God through leadership in music and liturgy. They are a leading choir in the Anglican tradition and have sung services and concerts all over the world.
