Careers

As we continuously grow in our mission to invite all to experience a joyful relationship with God, to share friendship with one another, and to make Jesus Christ known in the world, we are currently seeking additional staff members to join us in that work.Â 

At Trinity, we foster a workplace that is collaborative, supportive, and flexible. We welcome diversity of thought and life experience. We do not require staff members to be parishioners at Trinity, nor do we require staff members to be Episcopalians.

Applicants for any open position should submit a cover letter and resume to:

Trinity Episcopal Cathedral
Office of the Dean
1100 Sumter St.
Columbia, SC 29201

Or submit by email to Carole Carter, executive assistant to the dean, at carter@trinitysc.org.

Questions about our policies or open positions? Let us know!

Open Positions

Director of the Trinity Learning Center

We are seeking our next director of the Trinity Learning Center, a program of Trinity Cathedral. The TLC provides full-day childcare, as well as after-school care, drop-in programs and a summer camp.

Director of Music and Organist

We are seeking a key leader to continue the rich tradition of liturgical and musical excellence at Trinity Cathedral, and to consider how God is calling us to serve our community through our musical tradition even more faithfully and effectively.

Associate for Database Technologies and the Trinity Foundation

We are seeking a candidate with development experience in a religious or non-profit setting, and a proven ability to maintain donor and membership databases.

TLC Teacher Assistant/Floater

At Trinity Learning Center we are looking for someone who is full of excitement and eager to learn and interact with children.

Kitchen Coordinator

Oversee the overall day-to-day operations of the Trinity kitchen to support the food service operations of Trinity Cathedral and the Trinity Learning Center.
