Tidings Weekly Newsletter

Tidings is Trinity Cathedral’s weekly newsletter, delivered to your email inbox every Thursday morning. Here you’ll find information about upcoming events, important announcements, and celebrations in the life of our parish.

Submit Tidings & Bulletin Announcements

If you have an announcement for Tidings or our bulletin inserts, email the details to communications@trinitysc.org and attach documents (including artwork) no later than Tuesday at 5 p.m. If you need a registration form for your event, please request the form in the same email.

Announce a Birth or Death

If you have welcomed a new addition to your family, or have lost a family member, please let us know. Send this information directly to Allison Fowler, Trinity’s membership secretary.
