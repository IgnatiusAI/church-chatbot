Vestry

The vestry is responsible for the business affairs of the parish and represents the congregation in every area of decision-making.

Vestry members are elected at the annual parish meeting held in January and serve staggered three-year terms. Also serving on the vestry are the immediate past senior warden, the president of the Daughters of the Holy Cross and the president of the Trinity Foundation. The vestry meets on the fourth Thursday of each month.
